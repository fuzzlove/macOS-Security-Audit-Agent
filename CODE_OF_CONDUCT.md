# Code of Conduct

This project follows a simple standard: be respectful, be precise, and keep the discussion focused on the code and its impact.

## Expected Behavior

- Treat contributors and users with respect
- Keep technical critique grounded in evidence
- Avoid harassment, slurs, or personal attacks
- Avoid publishing private data unnecessarily

## Unacceptable Behavior

- harassment
- threats
- doxxing
- intentional disclosure of secrets or private data
- abuse of review or issue channels

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove content or restrict participation when behavior undermines the project or the safety of users and contributors.
